Announcing StrideOn — From the #VERYHackathon

The City Is Your Arena.

A decentralized, move-to-earn game that turns your morning run into a real-world, strategic game for territory. Claim your city, one step at a time.

Request Early Access View on GitHub

A New Breed of Fitness App

This isn't just about tracking miles. We've designed a game of strategy, skill, and real-time rivalry.

Paper.io Meets the Real World

As you run, you draw a trail on a live H3 hex grid. Close the loop to claim the territory you've enclosed. Bigger risks bring bigger rewards.

Tactical Real-Time Rivalry

Your active trail is a vulnerability. Nearby runners can sprint to physically cross your line, cutting your claim and creating intense, tactical chases.

On-Chain Fairness

The Very Network is our source of truth. Final territory claims, ranks, and rewards are recorded on-chain for transparent, tamper-proof results.

Built for Speed & Security

StrideOn uses a smart hybrid architecture to deliver a lag-free experience with trustless security.

⚡️ Lag-Free Experience

Our centralized game server, using Redis for caching, handles all the fast, real-time gameplay. This includes live GPS tracking and instant collision detection for breaking Strides, ensuring the game is always responsive.

🔗 Trustless Security

The Very Network is our secure trust layer. Our Smart Contract handles only the most critical actions: securely "banking" players' final scores and transparently distributing daily rewards in VERY tokens.

How You Play to Win

StrideOn is built around a simple, sustainable token loop tied directly to gameplay.

🏆 Daily Leaderboards

Capture the most territory to climb the daily leaderboard in your city and earn VERY token rewards.

🛡️ Shield

Protect your active trail for a short window to safely bank a huge territory claim.

👻 Ghost Mode

Go temporarily invisible on the map to secure large territories without being detected by rivals.
